What is
MoodMo?

MoodMo is a self-hosted and open-source mood tracking and journal application crafted for privacy and simplicity. With MoodMo, you can keep track of your mood and journal your thoughts, gaining invaluable insights into your emotional fluctuations and patterns.

Add Activities, Weather & More

Self-Host It!

With MoodMo you can harness the power of mood tracking and journaling while keeping your data private and secure in your own server or computer. Say goodbye to third-party platforms and hello to complete autonomy over your emotional insights.
